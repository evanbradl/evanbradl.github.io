Overview

My capstone project for University of Iowa was to develop an augmented reality scavenger hunt with several key components that are further outlined below. At the highest level, the project is a web-based application that will allow designated administrative accounts to create scavenger hunts. What separates our application from other scavenger hunt systems is the use of augmented reality. This will allow users to engage in and learn better from hunts using imaging, videos, and 3D dimensional figures that they can see in the real world through the lens of their cellular devices. While our primary customer in development was the University of Iowa College of Engineering, we designed our system to allow expansion to any other domain or entity that would benefit from an advanced technology scavenger hunt. While the specifics of rolling the application out at a commercial level did not fall within the scope of our project development, we designed the application to be flexible with these long-term goals in mind.
