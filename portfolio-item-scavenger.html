AR Scavenger Hunt

Web App

This project is currently in progress to be used by The University of Iowa's Engineering department.
The project is an Augmented Reality website featuring a scavenger hunt game aimed at engaging and educating new/prospective students about the University of Iowa and its facilities. As of now no well-rounded resource exists to teach new students about the University of Iowa campus/history. Tours may be offered at select times, however, at the student's convenience the only option may be to wander around and do their best to familiarize themselves. This website is intended to be accessed via portable devices with cameras, such as smart phones, tablets, or any other capable device. The student will then be able to follow through with an interactive scavenger hunt that will display additional information, pictures, or even videos when they reach the specified location. The location will be clearly marked with a poster, or some other form of display. This website will be initially geared towards the College of Engineering and will be used to supplement the scavenger hunt incoming freshmen complete each year.
